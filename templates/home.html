What Can You Do With Image Drive?
  • With Image Drive, you can upload images to your personal account
  • Images saved on your account can be downloaded to your local computer
  • Image Drive is designed to be a cloud service that anyone may use to house images if their computer does not have enough storage
  • Accepted image formats include: .jpg, .gif, .jpeg, .tiff
  • Image drive is also completely free; to create account, you must sign up with a valid email
  • Side Note: Image Drive is a coding project done by Ethan Y.
